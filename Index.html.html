How to read it

  • The calculator is now fixed to a two-week pay period, so no weekly toggle or weekly projection appears.
  • The wage guarantee uses engaged time only, not total online time.
  • The mileage line uses engaged miles only and multiplies them by $0.34.
  • The Prop 22 payout never drops below zero even when your base earnings already beat the floor.
  • The total projected pay card adds any Prop 22 top-up to your base ride earnings for the same biweekly period.
